Website Blacklisting

Enter websites to blacklist them during focus sessions and in general. Blacklisted websites will be blocked at all times, helping you stay focused.

Customizable Schedules

Set your daily focus schedule by selecting start and end times. During these hours, blacklisted websites will be automatically blocked. Outside these hours, blacklisted sites will be accessible.

Analytics

Track your daily focus time throughout the week. The chart updates automatically and persists your data even when you close the application.

Focus Timer

Set your work and break durations, then use the timer to maintain focused work sessions. The alarm will notify you when it's time to switch modes.
